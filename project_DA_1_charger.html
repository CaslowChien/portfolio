Data Analysis and Prediction for Electric Vehicle



This project, developed during my "Introduction to Big Data Analytics" course, consists of two main parts: 1. Analyzing data to confirm the growth trends and environmental benefits of electric vehicles (EVs), and 2. Predicting the required number of EV charging stations in specific regions using global data, serving as a prototype for future products. My contributions included full-stack web development, data analysis, DNN model creation, and data visualization.
