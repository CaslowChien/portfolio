Mental Health Trends Analysis: Taiwan Youth Focus



This project, part of a senior-year data competition, focused on youth mental health issues around the world. Our team collected relevant data, analyzed insights to identify potential causes and solutions, and presented clear relationships through visualization. I contributed to data collection, cleaning, analysis, visualization, and report writing, using Excel for charts and Tableau for data mapping.

My Contribution



  • Analyzed mental health trends among Taiwanese youth, uncovering data correlations and insights using Tableau for impactful visualizations.
  • Gained insights into the inherent value of official data, identifying data correlations, and uncovering potential reasons behind them.
