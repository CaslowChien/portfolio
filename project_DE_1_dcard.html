Data Scraper and Pipeline on Social Platform



For the 2020 National College Business Case competition, my team developed a data scraper and pipeline for Taiwan's leading college student social media platform. This automated solution reduced data collection time from 4 weeks to minutes, enabling us to efficiently analyze key insights for a company's market expansion strategy. Our project earned us a spot as finalists in the competition.
